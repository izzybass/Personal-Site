Interests

I am an observer. I marvel at the technological advancment of our age and contemplate on the collective feeling of insecurity. Our lives move at a heightened rate and with a high degree of variance. I think back to the recent financial crisis of 2008-2009 when average citizens lost significant portions of their savings and some billionaires were reduced to debtors.

Our collective feelings of euphoria eventually end with collective feelings of hopelessness. History is filled with people who abandoned hope in moments of crisis, only they missed the dawn of a new order that soon arose.

As a member of the investment community, I am constantly reminded of the volatility of our existence. I seek the insights of other disciplines such as history, philosophy and psychology to develop a more balance oulook of the world; a more sober or more optimistic outlook.
